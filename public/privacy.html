1. Who we are

GAIN ("we," "us") operates the AI governance platform at governainow.com. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have over it.

If you have questions, contact privacy@governainow.com.

2. Information we collect

2.1 Information you provide

  • Account data: name, email address, password hash (managed by Firebase Authentication), display name.
  • Company profile: company name, industry, size, workspace configuration.
  • Team data: names, emails, and roles of members you invite to your workspace.
  • Governance content: AI system inventory entries, risk register entries, intake forms, AIAs, checklist responses, policies, contracts, consent records, incident reports, and any documents or notes you upload.
  • Policy generation prompts: the text you submit to the AI policy drafting feature, which is transmitted to our model provider (Anthropic) for generation. Do not submit PHI, SSNs, payment card data, or other sensitive personal data in these prompts.
  • Support communications: messages, attachments, and metadata when you contact support.

2.2 Information we collect automatically

  • Usage data: pages viewed, features used, timestamps, referrer, user-agent, IP address.
  • Authentication tokens: managed by Firebase Authentication to keep you signed in.
  • Audit log: a record of actions taken in your workspace (who did what, when) — visible to your workspace admins.

2.3 Payment information

Billing is handled by Stripe. We do not store your full payment card number; Stripe handles that directly. We receive a payment token, subscription status, last-four digits, and billing address from Stripe.

3. How we use your information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Service.
  • To authenticate you and secure your account.
  • To process subscriptions and payments via Stripe.
  • To generate drafted policy content using the Anthropic API when you use the AI drafting feature.
  • To communicate with you about your account, billing, security, product changes, and support requests.
  • To detect, investigate, and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.
  • To analyze aggregate, de-identified usage patterns to improve the product.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your data to train our own AI models. We do not use Customer Data submitted to the Anthropic API to train Anthropic's public models (see Section 5).

4. Legal bases (EEA, UK, and Switzerland)

If you are in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, our legal bases for processing are:

  • Contract — to provide the Service you've subscribed to.
  • Legitimate interests — to secure our systems, prevent abuse, and improve the Service.
  • Legal obligation — to comply with tax, regulatory, and law-enforcement requirements.
  • Consent — where required (e.g., certain optional communications).

5. Sharing with sub-processors

We share personal data only with vetted third-party sub-processors who help us provide the Service. Current sub-processors are published at governainow.com/sub-processors. As of the last update:

  • Google Cloud / Firebase (US): hosting, authentication, database, cloud functions.
  • Anthropic (US): AI model inference for the policy drafting feature. Prompts and outputs sent via Anthropic's API are not used to train public Anthropic models; see Anthropic's API terms for current data handling.
  • Stripe (US): subscription billing and payment processing.
  • Brevo / Sendinblue (EU): transactional email delivery (password reset, team invites, notifications).
  • MochaHost (US): static marketing-page hosting; does not process authenticated Customer Data.

We execute data processing agreements with each sub-processor requiring protections substantially similar to this Policy.

6. Cookies and similar technologies

We use a minimal set of cookies and browser storage, limited to what the Service needs to function:

  • Firebase Authentication cookies/local storage — to keep you signed in.
  • Preference storage — to remember your theme and palette choices.
  • Stripe cookies — set during checkout; see Stripe's privacy notices for details.

We do not use third-party advertising or cross-site tracking cookies. If that changes, we will update this Policy and provide appropriate controls.

7. International transfers

GAIN is operated from the United States. When we transfer personal data from the EEA, UK, or Switzerland to the United States or other jurisdictions, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as the EU Standard Contractual Clauses, UK International Data Transfer Addendum, and the EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework (where applicable).

8. Data retention

  • Active workspaces: we retain Customer Data for as long as your subscription is active.
  • Cancelled workspaces: we make Customer Data available for export for 30 days after cancellation, then delete it from active systems. Encrypted backups may persist for up to 90 days before rotation.
  • Audit and financial records: we retain billing and audit records for up to 7 years to meet tax and accounting requirements.

9. Your rights

9.1 All users

  • Access your personal data via the in-app export feature or on request.
  • Correct inaccurate data via your account settings.
  • Delete your account and associated personal data via settings or by contacting us.
  • Port your data via our export feature (CSV / JSON on Pro and above).

9.2 EEA, UK, Switzerland residents (GDPR / UK GDPR)

You additionally have rights to object to processing, restrict processing, and lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority.

9.3 California residents (CCPA / CPRA)

You have rights to know, delete, correct, and limit the use of sensitive personal information. We do not sell or share personal information as those terms are defined under the CCPA/CPRA.

To exercise any right, contact privacy@governainow.com. We respond within 30 days (or 45 days where permitted by law) and will not discriminate against you for exercising a right.

10. Security

We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards including:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256 via Firebase/Google Cloud).
  • Role-based access controls within workspaces (owner, admin, editor, viewer).
  • Firestore security rules enforcing per-workspace data isolation.
  • Password hashing handled by Firebase Authentication.
  • Audit logging of material workspace actions.
  • Least-privilege access for our personnel.

No system is perfectly secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact security@governainow.com.

11. AI-specific notes

  • Policy generation transmits your prompt text to Anthropic's API. Do not include PHI, SSNs, payment card data, or other sensitive personal data in prompts.
  • No training on your data. Anthropic's API terms (as of the last update of this Policy) do not use your prompts or completions to train Anthropic's public models. We do not use your prompts or completions to train any model.
  • Generated Output is drafting material. It is not legal, compliance, medical, or financial advice. Have qualified professionals review Generated Output before relying on it.

12. Children

The Service is a B2B tool not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13 (or under the age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact privacy@governainow.com and we will delete it.

13. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you by email or in-app at least 30 days before the change takes effect. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent version.
