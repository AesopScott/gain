Frequently asked

Can I start on the free Starter plan and upgrade later?

Yes. Every new workspace starts on Starter. You can upgrade to Pro or Business anytime from the Billing page — the upgrade is prorated.

What's the difference between Pro and Business?

Business adds higher limits (35 systems vs 15, 25 users vs 10, 3 workspaces vs 1), SSO, custom branding, vendor BAA and contract tracking, and a faster support SLA. If you're in a regulated industry (healthcare, finance, education) or have more than ~15 AI systems, Business is the right pick.

Do you offer a discount for annual billing?

Yes — 8% off Pro, 10% off Business, 13% off Enterprise (about one free month on each tier). Toggle "Annual" above to see the annual prices.

Does GAIN sign a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) with my organization?

No. GAIN is not designed to store PHI — the product is a governance and documentation tool, and you should not upload patient data to it. We do not sign BAAs as a vendor. Instead, GAIN's Business and Enterprise plans include a BAA and vendor-contract tracking module where you upload and manage the BAAs you hold with your AI vendors (model providers, data processors, etc.), with renewal reminders and clause checklists aligned to HIPAA §164.308 and NIST AI RMF GOVERN 5.1.

Does AI-generated policy drafting use my data?

We use Anthropic's API for drafting. Your prompts and outputs are not used by Anthropic for training. No PHI should be sent to the drafting feature — it's intended for policy language, not patient data.

What happens if I cancel?

Your workspace downgrades to Starter at the end of the current billing period. All your data stays — just some features and higher limits are locked until you re-upgrade.

How do I enable SAML SSO, API access, or custom data residency?

SAML SSO (Okta / Azure AD / OneLogin), API access, and data residency options are Enterprise-tier features that we provision on a per-customer basis — there's no self-serve toggle. Google Workspace SSO is self-serve on Business and above. Submit a ticket or contact sales to scope the setup for your organization.
