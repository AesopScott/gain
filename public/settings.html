Data & deletion rights

Export a full copy of your workspace data at any time, or request permanent deletion of your workspace or account. All deletions are recorded against a verifiable Deletion Certificate.

Export workspace data

Downloads a ZIP containing one JSON file per collection in your workspace (inventory, risks, AIAs, policies, contracts, audit log, etc.) plus a manifest. Available to all members.

Export audit log only

Exports just the audit log — every governance action taken in this workspace — in either CSV or JSON format. The full Audit Log page has filters and search: open audit log →

Delete workspace

Permanently delete this workspace and all its data. The workspace is soft-deleted immediately and hard-deleted after a 30-day recovery window. Encrypted backups may persist up to 90 days after hard-delete before rotation. You'll receive a Deletion Certificate with a verification code by email. Only the workspace owner can perform this action.

Delete my account

Permanently delete your GAIN account. Your account is soft-deleted and sign-in is disabled immediately; hard-delete occurs after the 30-day recovery window. If you own any workspaces, you must transfer or delete them first. You'll receive a Deletion Certificate by email.
