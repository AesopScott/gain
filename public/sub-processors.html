Notification of changes

We update this page when we add, replace, or remove a sub-processor. For material changes, we provide at least 30 days' notice before the change takes effect.

To be notified by email of changes to this list, email privacy@governainow.com with the subject "Subscribe to sub-processor change notifications" and include the domain of the workspace you represent.

If you object to the addition or replacement of a sub-processor on reasonable grounds, contact us during the notice period and we will work with you in good faith. Where an objection cannot be resolved, you may terminate the affected portion of the Service per our Data Processing Agreement.
