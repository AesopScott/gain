Miscellaneous

A home for governance functions that don't yet warrant their own top-level nav item. Each tool below stands on its own — pick one to get started.

Available tools

Incident Active
Incident Response Tracking
Log, triage, and resolve AI-related incidents — model failures, bias events, prompt-injection attempts, data leaks, or any adverse impact that needs a documented response. Satisfies NIST AI RMF MANAGE 4.1 and ISO 42001 A.8.3.
Training Active
Training Tracker
Build a course catalog, schedule classes by period, import your employee roster, and track enrollment and completion. Satisfies NIST AI RMF GOVERN 6.1 and HIPAA workforce training requirements.
Contracts Active
Vendor Contracts & BAAs
Upload and track Business Associate Agreements, Data Processing Agreements, and AI vendor contracts. Monitor clause coverage, expiration dates, and due-diligence reviews. Satisfies HIPAA BAA requirements and NIST AI RMF GOVERN 5.1.
Audit Active
Audit Log
Append-only record of every governance action in this workspace — who did what, when, and on which record. Filter by action type or actor. Export to CSV. Satisfies NIST AI RMF GOVERN 1.7 and ISO 42001 A.9.1.
Consent Active
Consent Records
Log and track individual consent for AI-driven data processing. Pre-built templates for HIPAA, FERPA, GDPR, GLBA, CCPA, ECOA, NAIC, and EU AI Act. Each record is timestamped and tied to the recording editor.
Disclosure Active
Public Disclosure Tracker
Track where and when AI systems have been publicly disclosed — transparency notices, NYC LL144 bias-audit postings, EU AI Act filings, and voluntary statements. Linked from checklist items that require public disclosure.
